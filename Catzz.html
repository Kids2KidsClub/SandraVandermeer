What Are Cats?

Cats are furry animals that are pets. Cats are animals that are cuddly and they are really playful. They have four legs and they use a litter box for a bathroom. Cats do need a lot of things because they think they are somthing like royals! They need a bed, plenty of toys, a litter box, and a lot of attention. If you want a cat, then you have to have atleast one person at home 24/7, if not then the cat will go crazy and will tear up the furniture. Cats can have all different kinds of fur, like short, long, and maybe even none! Cats have very sensitive ears and can hear things from over a distance of 10 feet! Their ears have very little fur and cats enjoy it when you rub the inside with two fingers. Cats have a pur that can be heard within a distannce of 5 feet. Cats use their throats to pur and the pur can be heard best when you have your ear near their body. Cats are really pretty animals and theiri fur can come in different colors like black, white, brown, and grey. Cats are dainty and rarely need a bath because to cats, bathing means licking themselves. Cats hate getting wet with water. Cats are nocturnal creatures which means that they are most active at night. I once found my cat opening my door in the middle of the night, and that woke me up. Cats mostly spend their day sleeping and they sleep for about 19 hours a day! Cats mostly do only 2-3 things a day. Eat, sleep, and play. Cats are easy to take care of and when they get older, they can start to sleep 20-21 hours a day and only do 2 things. Eat and sleep. Cats have sharp claws that curve out of their paws and their claws are really sharp. They can tear up leather like it is wet paper.

How Do You Give Your Cat A Bath?

  1. First you have to catch your cat. Catching your cat for a bath will be tricky because cats dont like baths. They hate getting their fur wet with water.
  2. Once you catch your cat, then you can put them in warm water in the bathtub.
  3. Once done, to rinse, pour warm water over the cat.
  4. Be careful on this part. Carefully scrub soap onto the cats fur.
  5. After scrubbing the soap onto the cats fur, wash it out by puoring warm water onto the cat.
  6. The cat will want to run away so with a strong grip (not to strong), dry your cat with a towel. Do not use a hairdryer.
